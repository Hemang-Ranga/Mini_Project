About us

We are a team of students from IIIT Dharwad, working on a website that enables users to convert voice to text. Our aim is to create a tool that is both user-friendly and effective, allowing users to easily and accurately transcribe spoken words. With the mentorship of our professor Dr. Deepak KT Sir, we have been able to develop a tool that we believe will be of great value to a wide range of users. We are excited to share our project with the world and look forward to hearing feedback from our users.
